> My personal brand is centered around the idea of creating a unique identity that reflects my values and personality.
> The logo for my brand is a bold and modern representation of my name Esaú. It consists of the letter M, which is horizontally inverted to form the letter E, standing for my full name Esaú Morais.
> Overall, my personal brand is a representation of who I am as a person and as a professional.
> It showcases my creativity, individuality, and my commitment to creating a brand that is uniquely mine.
